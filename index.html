Welcome to my universe

I created my first videos at the age of 12, driven by a simple desire to create. Around the same time, I discovered scuba diving thanks to my father — a passion that, like video, has stayed with me ever since.Two years ago, I turned to photography and found the same intensity and sense of fulfillment as I did with video. My many travels, often solo, have helped shape my perspective and refine my artistic approach. Today, I’m looking to take this journey to a professional level, with a focus on underwater photography.
